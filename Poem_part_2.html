You don't say! Dignity and Respect have been evicted from this place, overshadowed by Distrust and fueled by rampant slander. And Respect? She fled for her life in a dark night, never to be seen or heard of again. Their house is now the property of the Richmonds and the Disparities. Oh! Can you show me the house of the Laughter family? Ah! You must be talking about Laughter, the sister of Happy. You guessed right. They are the ones I am speaking of! You are so behind the times! They have sold their house to Mrs Lament many years ago and have left town. Who is that you speak of? The Lovejoys? No sir. Since the death of Belief, they have never been heard of anymore. Their house is locked up and deserted. Well, is no one of the good old days here any more? Speaking of the old residents, do you see the five story building on the right? Yes, that is the house of Mr. Sadness. It was just a single story house before. Right beside that, Mrs Pain lives on the top floor of the third story building. The foundation of that house is for ten stories, we hear! Do you see the house by the field. That belongs to the family of the Cheats. It was just a small hut in the olden days, but it is like a palace these days. Mistrust rents a floor in there. And the house all the down there, by the little pond yonder, that is Mrs. Poverty's. That one, surprisingly, is just the same as it always was. And who may you be, sir? Oh, my friends! My friends! Didn't you recognize me? I am Destiny. You may not have seen me before, but might have heard of me. And who might you two be? I am Life and she is Peace. You know, we have been waiting for you for so long. Please stay with us in our community from now on. What is that you say? Where will you stay? Just tell us which house you would prefer and it would be yours. But don't leave, I need you. I am sorry, my friend. Where there is no Faith, Love, Honesty, Dignity or Respect, how could Life and Peace be present there? No, we will move along my friend. But rest assured, we shall neither call any place our home, nor shall we stop until we find our worthy friends, who give meaning to life and let peace dwell. May be in another place, in another time, we will meet again in the presence of our truest and most valued friends! But until then, Good Bye!
